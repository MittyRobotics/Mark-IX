Introduction

Logging during matches will be helpful for fine tuning the robot and for seeing what the CRIO thinks it is doing. Versus what you told it to do...

Installation

Step 1: Make folder "log" in the root folder of the CRIO

Step 2: Create comp.txt and move it to the log folder you just created on the CRIO

Step 3: Test it.

Step 4: There is no step 4. Move along.
